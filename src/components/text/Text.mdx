## Sizes

```jsx preview center
<Text size="sm">
  Anything smaller than body text. E.g. "forgot your password?" prompts
</Text>
```

```jsx preview center
<Text>Body text. Most text in the app will use this. </Text>
```

```jsx preview center
<Text size="lg">Large, non-heading text. E.g. a drop quote in an article</Text>
```
