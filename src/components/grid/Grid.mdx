---
title: Grid
component: Grid
category: Layout
---

The `Grid` component is analagous to `Box` but additionally sets the rendering context of its children to `display: grid`.

```jsx preview
<Grid
  css={{
    gap: '$3',
    gridAutoFlow: 'column',
    gridTemplateColumns: '33% 33% 33%'
  }}
>
  <Text css={{ bg: '$tonal200', gridColumn: '1', gridRow: '1', p: '$2' }}>
    Hello
  </Text>
  <Text css={{ bg: '$tonal200', gridColumn: '2', gridRow: '2', p: '$2' }}>
    Hello
  </Text>
  <Text css={{ bg: '$tonal200', gridColumn: '3', gridRow: '1', p: '$2' }}>
    Hello
  </Text>
</Grid>
```
